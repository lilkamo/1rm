Attempt strategy
1️⃣
Opener at 92–93%. A lift you could do on a bad day. Conservative. Sets you up mentally. Never miss an opener.
2️⃣
Second attempt at 97–99%. Just below your old max or matching it. This is where you confirm your current level. Only jump here if the opener moved well.
3️⃣
Third attempt is the PR. Add 2.5–5kg above your previous max. Only take this if the second felt like a true 97–98% effort. If second was a grind, stay there — that's your new max.
➕
If it still feels easy after 3. Rest 8–10 mins, take one more attempt. Add 2.5kg only. After that, stop — you've found your max.
Jump sizes
🟢
Opener → second: jump 5–7.5% of your 1RM.
🟡
Second → third: jump 2.5–5kg only. Small and deliberate.
🔴
Never jump more than 5kg on a third attempt. Big jumps on a max effort is how you miss lifts that are actually in you.
On the day
⏱
Rest 4–6 mins between attempts. Full recovery. Do not rush.
🍽
Eat a full maintenance day. Carbs at breakfast and lunch. Don't train fasted on 1RM day.
💧
Sip water between attempts. Not cold, not too much. Stay hydrated, not bloated.
🧠
Same warm-up every time. Bar → 40% → 55% → 65% → 72.5% → 80% → opener. Trust the routine.
😤
Miss a lift? Take 2.5kg off and re-attempt once. One retry only. If you miss twice at the same weight, that's your max for today.
